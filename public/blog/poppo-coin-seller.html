Poppo Live App

Poppo Live is a live-streaming application that helps to build connections worldwide. This application is available on Android and iOS.

Poppo Live deals in their virtual currency as Poppo points and Poppo coins:

Poppo Live Coins:

Poppo coins are the virtual currency that the users transfer to their favorite hosts as a token of appreciation. The users get these coins through recharge. The value of the coins in recharge depends on the source of purchase.

Poppo Points:

Poppo Points are the received income of the hosts. These points can be earned through live streaming, Party Room, PK, and daily tasks. The conversion value of the points is 10000 Points = $1. These points can be exchanged with the Poppo coins as well.

How to Become a Coin Seller on Poppo - Requirements

All the agencies can start coin trading in Poppo Live anytime. The agency needs to follow either of the two requirements to become an official coin seller.

  • Must maintain a minimum of 5 active hosts with >= 6 hours of live or party in a week.
  • Recharge or exchange coins of 2000 USD in one transaction and store them in the agent’s account.
OR
  • Recharge or exchange of 6000 USD equivalent coins in one transaction and stored in the agent’s account.

How to Trade in Poppo Live

The agency, that wants to start the coin trading, will purchase the coins directly from the Poppo Live app and store them in their agency’s account. Whenever any user or agency approaches the coin trader, they will sell the coins to the user with a slight profit margin.

Poppo Live Salary - Profit Margin for Coin Seller

  • Increase 10%-30% profit, by selling coins to users.
  • Earn 7%-14% profit by selling coins to agents

Points to Remember to be a Coin Seller

  • Every Poppo Agency has the option to start coin-trading anytime. Click here to learn more about Poppo Agency
  • A user can bargain the coin price from the agency. They can check for the top coin seller on the Poppo App and message them directly.

  • The agency should keep the stable selling amount every month to keep the coin-seller feature, otherwise, this feature will be canceled.

Poppo Live Recharge Coins

Recharge Coins through Epay or USDT

To recharge coins through the Poppo App, follow these steps:

  • 1. Log in to your profile and click on the "Coin Trading" option.
  • 2. Choose Epay or USDT as your payment method.
  • 3. The minimum amount you can top-up is $100.

Points Exchange

The Poppo Coin Seller can exchange earning points for coins and transfer them to other users.

  • 1. Make sure to have at least 100,000 points.
  • 2. Go to "Coin Trading" and select "Exchange."
  • 3. Enter the amount to convert.
  • 4. Complete the captcha and click "Confirm".

The exchange rate will be between 92,000 and 95,000 coins, depending on the agency’s rate.
