Poppo Host Ranking Rules

Host Live Ranking Rules

  • A host must do the live-streaming for >= 1 hour.
  • The Poppo Host achieves the rank based on the live-stream earnings of the last 3 days excluding the platform task and ranking rewards.
  • The rewards must be claimed every day otherwise, it will expire automatically.

Host Wish Ranking Rules

  • Hosts are ranked based on how many wishes they fulfill.
  • The top 20 hosts get rewards every minute, plus a daily bonus of 960,000 points.
  • If a host doesn’t claim their reward, their wish count keeps growing.
  • Hosts can claim their rewards at any time. After claiming, their points and wish count reset, and the leaderboard updates.

How to Get a Wish

  • Click the “Wish” button in the top left corner of the live room to receive a wish gift.
  • Every hour, the system gives a random wish gift to the host.
  • Users or hosts can spend 5000 coins to convert the wish gift, or users can fulfill wishes by sending gifts.

Host Tips Ranking

  • Hosts are ranked based on the tips they earn from gaming.
  • To calculate tips: (Winning Amount — Bet Amount) * 1%.

Notes:

  • Rewards need to be claimed manually each day or they will expire.
  • The rankings are updated every Monday at midnight (UTC+8).
  • Cheating will result in a deduction of rewards.
